NCI Gadi HPC SU Calculator

Estimated SU Usage

Formula:
rate × (ncpus_request × max[1, (ncpus_per_node/mem_per_node) × (mem_request/ncpus_request)]) × hours / ncpus_per_node
For GPU jobs: rate × (gpus_requested / gpus_per_node) × nodes × hours
